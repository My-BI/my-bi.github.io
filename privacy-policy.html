Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

MyBI is local-first. The app runs on your own computer and your business data — datasets, models, dashboards and project files — stays on your machine. We don't upload it, we can't see it, and nothing leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export, publish or share it.

The short version. The free MyBI app needs no account and works fully offline — we collect nothing. We only process limited account information if you sign in to an optional enterprise/organisation feature, and we only handle data you deliberately send when you use an optional cloud feature (e.g. publishing a dashboard or using a cloud AI engine). We never sell your data.

1. Who we are

“MyBI”, “we”, “us” refers to the maker of the MyBI desktop application and its companion apps (MyBI Publisher Studio, MyBI Enterprise Studio, MyBI Forensic) and this website (my-bi.github.io). You can reach us at mybusinessintel@gmail.com.

2. The free MyBI app — no account, fully offline

The core MyBI app does not require an account and is designed to run without a network connection. Your data is processed by an on-device engine and stored in portable project files (.mybi and related formats) on your own disk. We receive no telemetry about your data, your usage, or the contents of your projects.

3. Information we do not collect

  • Your datasets, tables, queries, models, dashboards or canvases.
  • The contents of files you import or projects you build.
  • Behavioural analytics inside the app about how you use it.

4. Information we process — only for optional features

Some features are optional and only run when you turn them on. For those, we process the minimum necessary:

Account & identity (enterprise / organisation sign-in)

If you use an organisation feature that requires signing in, we process your account identity — your name, email address, profile picture (as provided by your identity provider) and your organisation membership and role. Authentication uses your chosen provider (e.g. Google or Microsoft) and is handled on our behalf by Supabase (our authentication and database processor). We use this only to sign you in, link you to your organisation, and apply your role and licence.

Licensing

For paid plans we record your subscription status, tier and the organisation it belongs to, so we can grant the right features. Payments are handled by our payment provider; we do not store full card details.

Optional cloud features you trigger

  • Publishing / sharing — when you choose to publish a dashboard (for example to a static site or a MyBI Server), only the data you select is sent, and only at your instruction.
  • Cloud AI engines — MyBI's assistant runs on-device by default. If you explicitly connect an external cloud AI provider, the prompts and context you submit are sent to that provider under its own terms; MyBI surfaces this clearly before anything leaves the app.

5. This website

This marketing site is served as static pages by GitHub Pages. It stores your theme preference (light/dark/system) locally in your browser and does not require an account. Standard web-server request logs may be kept by the hosting provider for security and reliability.

6. How we use information

  • To authenticate you and provide the features you sign in for.
  • To manage organisations, roles and licences.
  • To operate, secure and improve the service and respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Our legal bases (UK/EU GDPR) are: contract (to provide features you request), legitimate interests (to secure and operate the service) and consent (for optional features you switch on).

7. Sharing & processors

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data only with the processors that run optional features:

SupabaseAuthentication & identity, organisation/licence database (enterprise features)
Identity providers (Google, Microsoft)Sign-in — they share basic profile (name, email) per your consent

We may disclose information if required by law.

8. International transfers

Our processors may store data outside your country. Where that happens, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) as required by law.

9. Security

On-device data is protected by your operating system and, for sensitive items, device-bound encryption. Exports can be sealed with standard, audited cryptography (Ed25519 signatures, SHA-256 hash chains). Account data held by our processors is protected by their security controls and row-level access rules.

10. Retention

Your project data lives on your device for as long as you keep it. Account and licence data is kept while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards to meet legal and accounting requirements, then deleted or anonymised.

11. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request to access, correct, export or delete the account information we hold, and object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email mybusinessintel@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority (in the UK, the ICO).

12. Children

MyBI is not directed to children and is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll change the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide a more prominent notice.

14. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email mybusinessintel@gmail.com.
