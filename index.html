What's inside

From raw files to a shared dashboard

Every capability is a self-contained block over one typed API — lightweight core, no bloat.

Get data

CSV, JSON, Parquet & spreadsheets, plus SQL databases & REST APIs. A guided Get-Data wizard with live preview and visual transforms — your original file is never modified.

Model & formulas

Build relationships visually with data-verified auto-detect, then write measures in a clear formula language — ~80 verified functions with autocomplete and live validation.

One Calc Engine

Every number runs through a single compute core. A pie slice that reads 95% reads 95% everywhere — verified by golden-number tests, by construction.

Charts & canvas

40 chart kinds across two native engines — bar to sankey, calendar, geo maps and 3D — on a zoomable canvas with drag-resize cards, cross-filtering, multi-sheet tabs, slicers, KPI tiles and themes. Publish once — it auto-scales to any screen.

Analytics & AI

Trends, forecasts and correlation on top of the engine — plus a native, on-device AI assistant that chats about your data and documents. Every data access is consent-gated, and nothing is sent to a cloud.

Extend everything

Signed plugins, a colour-palette marketplace, and a sandboxed SDK for custom blocks and Python / R scripts — third parties use the very same API.

Trust & control

Your data stays yours

MyBI runs on your machine, end to end — no account, no cloud, no telemetry. Nothing leaves your computer unless you choose to export it.

Numbers that reconcile

Every figure runs through one Calc Engine, so a total reads the same in every visual — checked by golden-number tests, not taken on faith.

No lock-in

Your projects live in portable .mybi files — back them up, move them between machines and keep them, with or without MyBI.

Genuine plugins

Every plugin carries an Ed25519 signature the app checks on install — so you know each one is authentic and untampered before it runs.

Sandboxed by design

Plugins run isolated, with declared permissions and no network by default — you see and control exactly what each one can touch.

Fully offline

No sign-up, no sync, no tracking. Open the app and work — everything happens on your computer, even with the network off.

Plugins & palettes

Extend MyBI from the in-app marketplace — add charts, connectors and colour palettes, each installed only with your permission.

Private by design. MyBI keeps your data on your device — no account to create, nothing uploaded, nothing shared. Share a dashboard live from your own server, as a portable encrypted snapshot, or as a static page — in every case only aggregated results leave; raw rows never do.
