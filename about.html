About

Business intelligence that stays close to home.

MyBI is a business intelligence platform for macOS, Windows and Linux. It turns raw data into dashboards, deep analytics and geospatial intelligence — without sending a single row to a server. Your data stays on your machine, with no account and no cloud.

The idea

Powerful analytics shouldn't cost you your data.

Most BI tools ask you to upload everything to a cloud you don't control, then trust a black box with the answer. MyBI takes the opposite stance: the engine runs on your own computer, the project file is yours, and nothing leaves the machine unless you explicitly export it.

It is built block-first — Importer, Semantic model, Calc Engine, Charts, Canvas and Analytics each do one job well and compose into a workspace. And every number runs through a single Calc Engine, so a total reads the same in every visual — checked, never assumed.

Private & offline

Runs fully offline. No account, no upload — your data lives on your device.

No lock-in

Your projects live in portable .mybi files — back up, move and keep them, with or without MyBI.

Block-first

Composable blocks — import, model, calculate, chart and lay out — that fit together.

Accountable

Signatures and exports use standard, audited cryptography — accountable end to end, no black box.

The MyBI family

One app, two companions

The platform you build in, plus two focused desktop tools for the people who publish plugins and the people who have to account for what left the building.

MyBI App

The BI app itself. Import data, model it in the Semantic layer, calculate with the Calc Engine, and build dashboards on an infinite canvas — charts, deep analytics and geospatial intelligence, all offline, all on your machine. Free for everyone, on macOS, Windows and Linux.

MyBI Publisher Studio Trusted by MyBI

A desktop signer for third-party plugin and palette authors. Generate your own Ed25519 keypair, sign your plugin locally, and submit your public key for countersignature — so your work ships with the “Trusted by MyBI” badge after review. Your private key never leaves your machine.

MyBI Forensic Studio Coming soon

A planned companion for security and compliance teams. It opens MyBI's encrypted, forensic exports — a content key sealed to your organisation with X25519, the body under AES-256-GCM — and verifies the Ed25519 SHA-256 hash-chained audit log, proving a bundle hasn't been tampered with and showing the disclosed device fingerprint behind every export.
